## Basic formatting

Markdown keeps writing simple while still giving you useful structure.

### Checklist

- Use headings to organize your ideas.
- Add lists when you need quick summaries.
- Keep paragraphs short for readability.

### Quote

> Good writing is clear thinking made visible.

### Links and emphasis

You can add [links](https://pages.github.com/), `inline code`, **bold text**, and *emphasis* without leaving the editor.
